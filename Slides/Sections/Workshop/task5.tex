\subsection{Analysis}
\begin{frame}{Analysis}
We need to perform analysis to ensure our simulations have run correctly.\vspace{1cm}

You should always check:
\begin{itemize}
	\item Has the energy converged during minimisation?
	\item Is the temperature correct?
	\item Is the volume constant during NVT ensembles?
	\item Is the energy stable during simulations?
	\item Is the protein stable during simulations?
\end{itemize}
\end{frame}
